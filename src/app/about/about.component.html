Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) serves as the Principal Financial Institution for promotion, financing and development of the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME sector) and co-ordination of the functions of the various Institutions engaged in similar activities. SIDBI operates various schemes for both Indirect Finance/Refinance and Direct Finance to create a multiplier effect and increase the flow of credit to MSMEs. Besides finance schemes, SIDBI takes up various activities/initiatives/schemes for encouraging entrepreneurship, enterprise creations and growth of MSMEs. SIDBI also promotes/supports various institutions which are engaged in financing and development of MSMEs. SIDBI has been actively developing various digital solutions to power the MSME ecosystem such as ‘GST Sahay’, ‘Udyam Assist Platform’ and now, has partnered with Jocata to launch the country’s first MSME-specific Economic Activity Index by leveraging India’s Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI).

Jocata (a subsidiary of BillDesk) is a technology company that delivers credit intelligence to the Banking and Financial Services industry. Founded in 2010, Jocata specializes in digital lending using credit automation, smart decisioning and intelligent monitoring. The highly configurable, purpose-built, low code platform from Jocata powers enterprise lending programs in Retail, Agri and MSME segments for Banks and NBFCs and also operates in a B2B SaaS model to enable Embedded Finance.

MSMEs who are at the core of India's growth story have a constant need for right capital. Traditionally, it has been difficult for lenders to confidently lend to this segment due to lack of reliable data, legacy underwriting models and high acquisition and servicing costs. Jocata solves for these problems in a unified manner through its digital lending platform and SaaS offerings.

In an industry first, Jocata built an AI/ML powered MSME score - SME DNA- which captures the quantitative performance as well as behavioral aspects of a small business. The score has been adopted by several institutions for their onboarding, assessment and monitoring decisions.

Jocata Sumpoorn is a part of this effort to provide a data-driven understanding of MSMEs to improve credit access for the sector and fuel its growth.
