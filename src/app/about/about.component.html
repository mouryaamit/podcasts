Index Working Group

Working Group comprising the two organisations behind Sumpoorn, and external members for the ongoing management and operational aspects of delivering the Index in the public realm.

Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) serves as the Principal Financial Institution for promotion, financing and development of the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME sector) and co-ordination of the functions of the various Institutions engaged in similar activities. SIDBI operates various schemes for both Indirect Finance/Refinance and Direct Finance to create a multiplier effect and increase the flow of credit to MSMEs. Besides finance schemes, SIDBI takes up various activities/initiatives/schemes for encouraging entrepreneurship, enterprise creations and growth of MSMEs. SIDBI also promotes/supports various institutions which are engaged in financing and development of MSMEs. SIDBI has been actively developing various digital solutions to power the MSME ecosystem such as ‘GST Sahay’, ‘Udyam Assist Platform’ and now, has partnered with Jocata to launch the country’s first MSME-specific Economic Activity Index by leveraging India’s Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI).

Jocata, a wholly owned subsidiary of BillDesk (IndiaIdeas), is a fintech solution provider that aids financial institutions in achieving their Digital Transformation objectives. Jocata powers digital lending, analytics and, financial crime compliance for marquee public and private banks and NBFCs.

As a platform partner for a spectrum of lenders, Jocata found that while technology has proved to be a key enabler, the lack of comprehensive and authentic data poses a major hurdle in bridging the MSME credit gap. This led to the creation of Jocata GST DNA, an MSME credit score that leverages consent-led data from GSTIN and holistically captures the business’ performance. The score has been back tested on over 50,000 MSMEs at leading institutions, who have adopted it for MSME acquisition, underwriting and monitoring.

Jocata Sumpoorn is an extension of this effort to provide a data-driven and timely understanding of MSMEs to further credit penetration to this sector and fuel the MSME powerhouse of the Indian economy.
