Jocata Sumpoorn and SME DNA have been recognized with the prestigious Product Innovation of the Year award at the Global SME Finance Forum 2024, held in Sao Paulo, Brazil. Jocata Sumpoorn in association with SIDBI was showcased at Citizen Stack - the first United Nations Conference on Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI). The Index, a shared value good between the financial ecosystem and the MSME ecosystem, was created as a public-private partnership between a fintech that brings innovation to the table and the public sector that brings scale. Jocata’s Platform+ approach to MSME credit growth and Jocata Sumpoorn index presented to International Finance Corporation (IFC) senior leaders from 100+ geographies. The Inclusive Finance India Report 2023, acknowledges Sumpoorn's role as a stakeholder in facilitating MSME financing and brings out the index's potential to address the information gaps hindering MSME credit access. Jocata presented “Sumpoorn” India’s first MSME Economic Activity Index in association with SIDBI at the Global Partnership for Financial Inclusion (GPFI) meeting under the G20 Presidency at the Global SME Finance Forum, managed by IFC (International Finance Corporation). SIDBI-Jocata Sumpoorn index presented as a key enabler for MSME growth in emerging economies Jocata launches “Sumpoorn” India’s first MSME Economic Activity Index in association with SIDBI to further MSME development by enabling strategic action and credit inclusion efforts. Unlocking Economic Potential: Digital Public Infrastructure for a $10 Trillion India – Jocata Sumpoorn as an Example of Privately Provisioned Public Infrastructure. The Inclusive Finance India Report 2025 features Jocata Sumpoorn in its chapter “State of MSME Finance in India”, recognising its role in MSME health assessment within India’s integrated financial infrastructure.
