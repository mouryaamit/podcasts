As part of India’s presentation to the world at Citizen Stack - The First United Nations Conference in New York on DPI (Digital Public Infrastructure), the Jocata Sumpoorn Index - a unique usecase of DPI - private infrastructure that is publicly provisioned, stands as a testament to this synergy. Jocata presented “Sumpoorn” India’s first MSME Economic Activity Index in association with SIDBI at the Global Partnership for Financial Inclusion (GPFI) meeting under the G20 Presidency at the Global SME Finance Forum, managed by IFC (International Finance Corporation). Jocata launches “Sumpoorn” India’s first MSME Economic Activity Index in association with SIDBI to further MSME development by enabling strategic action and credit inclusion efforts. The Inclusive Finance India Report 2023, acknowledges Sumpoorn's role as a stakeholder in facilitating MSME financing and brings out the index's potential to address the information gaps hindering MSME credit access.
