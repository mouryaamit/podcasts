A first-of-its-kind Index from Jocata – one of India’s most trusted Digital Lending Platform providers, in association with SIDBI – the Principal Financial Institution for MSME development, that truly represents the state of our MSMEs that contribute more than a quarter of India’s overall Gross Value Added (GVA) and more than 40% of total exports, reflected through actual sales data.

Why Build an MSME Index?

Available macro indices capture business expectations and sentiment. There is no index that captures the lifeblood of the Indian economy – MSMEs. Sumpoorn is an attempt to bridge this gap.

Use GST Returns to calculate MSME TTM sales for current month (TTMc) and previous month (TTMp).

For each GSTIN, calculate delta of monthly sales on a YoY basis, represent relative to TTMp; thereafter remove outliers.

Calculate delta of growing and declining MSMEs combined with their amplitude of sales.

Compute the relative amplitude adjusted diffusion index.

Algorithmically scale for values ranging from 0 to 1.

Index Methodology

Jocata Sumpoorn is a 'relative amplitude adjusted diffusion index' generated using monthly GST sales data of credit-seeking MSMEs.

The significance of the MSME sector for the Indian economy is a well acknowledged fact. However, data paucity on the MSME sector often becomes a challenge for industry members and policymakers.

We, at SIDBI, have been constantly engaged in the development of the sector and have been partnering with institutions to bridge the information asymmetry to enable informed decision-making by stakeholders. It is in this spirit that we have created Sumpoorn with Jocata, that carries an index which measures the economic activity of MSMEs by leveraging GST data.

The underlying authentic data of GST provides credibility to the Index. With time, the same can be expanded to include more dimensions. I hope the publication shall be of value to the readers and provide a rich perspective on the prevalent trends of the MSME sector.

The Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) and technology stacks developed by public and private institutions are allowing Indian MSMEs to evolve at a rapid pace. An understanding of this sector that is fact-based and dynamic has become critical for their sustained growth, especially through formal credit access.

As a digital transformation partner for marquee lenders in the country, Jocata has been deeply engaged in providing behavioural insights on MSME entities and powering near-instant credit decisioning and faster disbursals.

With the belief that a high-frequency indicator will help address the prevailing MSME knowledge gap, Jocata, in association with SIDBI, presents Sumpoorn, India's first and only economic activity index of credit-seeking MSMEs. With only a four-week reporting cycle, we believe Sumpoorn can effectively, and in a timely manner, aid various stakeholders at financial institutions, policy makers, economists and researchers in strategically directing development and credit efforts to fuel the growth of MSMEs across the country.
