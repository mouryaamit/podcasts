Why Build an MSME Index?

Available macro indices capture business expectations and sentiment. There is no index that captures the lifeblood of the Indian economy – MSMEs. Sumpoorn is an attempt to bridge this gap.

An MSME economic activity index is vital for

Lenders

To direct developmental and commercial capital to MSMEs

Policy makers

To create tailored policies to propel MSME growth & development

Economists and Researchers

To study policy and credit flow impact on the segment
