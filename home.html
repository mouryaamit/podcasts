Why Build an MSME Specific Index?

Unreliable, sporadic, and opinion or survey-based data indices and analytical reports are an incomplete measure to duly capture the state of our MSMEs. The persistent data gap impacts lenders’ ability to lend and the lack of dynamic information prevents policy makers, researchers, and institutions from understanding the onground issues and formulating effective development plans. 

Minds Behind the Index

Jocata’s inhouse team of credit experts and data scientists, guided by our patrons - SIDBI, advisors and senior economists, have developed Sumpoorn, a statistical measure to gauge MSME business activity levels by using anonymised and aggregated GST sales data of credit-seeking MSMEs. We have patiently tracked the index over the past 4 years to ensure its ability to represent the MSME economy and capture the impact of macroeconomic conditions.

Index Methodology and Frequency

Actual monthly sales data reflected in GSTN returns of credit-seeking MSMEs is used to calculate the delta of percentage of growing and declining GSTINs and combined with amplitude of change in turnover to generate Jocata Sumpoorn. The Index will be released monthly with only a four-week reporting cycle after the reference month ends (The Index for August will be published in the 1st week of October). Read more
