*GVA Growth %
Growth in Gross Value Added at current prices. GVA is a primary measure in national income statistics, defined as the value of output less the value of intermediate consumption.
How to read Jocata Sumpoorn with
other Macro Indices?

While Jocata Sumpoorn is an MSME specific fact-based Index, readers may want to interpret the Index movement with other published Indices to unearth different dimensions at play in the Indian economy. It is important to understand the construct and scope of each Index before reading the Indices in conjunction.
