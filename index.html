India’s only economic activity index of credit seeking MSMEs - Jocata “Sumpoorn”

Explore Index Features

A unique initiative that leverages India's Digital Public Infrastructure(DPI) by Jocata - one of India’s most trusted Digital Lending Platform providers, in association with SIDBI - the Principal Financial Institution for the growth and development of MSMEs.

Why Build an MSME Index?

Available macro indices measure production activity or capture business expectations and sentiment. There is no index that captures the lifeblood of the Indian economy – MSMEs.

An MSME economic activity index is vital for

Lenders

To direct developmental and commercial capital to MSMEs

Policy makers

To create tailored policies to propel MSME growth & development

Economists and Researchers

To study policy and credit flow impact on the segment

Minds Behind the Index

A team of credit experts, data scientists and senior economists have built and tracked the Index over the past 4 years to ensure its ability to represent the MSME economy and capture the impact of macroeconomic conditions. Read More

Index Methodology

Jocata Sumpoorn is a 'relative amplitude adjusted diffusion index' generated using monthly GST sales data of credit-seeking MSMEs.

Read More

The Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) and technology stacks developed by public and private institutions are allowing Indian MSMEs to evolve at a rapid pace. An understanding of this sector that is fact-based and dynamic has become critical for their sustained growth, especially through formal credit access.

As a digital transformation partner for marquee lenders in the country, Jocata has been deeply engaged in harnessing Goods and Services Tax (GST) data to provide deep insights on MSME entities and powering near-instant credit decisioning and faster disbursals.

With the belief that a high-frequency indicator will help address the prevailing MSME knowledge gap, Jocata, in association with SIDBI, presents “Sumpoorn”, India's first and only economic activity index of credit-seeking MSMEs. With only a four-week reporting cycle, we believe Sumpoorn can effectively, and in a timely manner, aid various stakeholders at financial institutions, policy makers, economists and researchers in strategically directing development and credit efforts to fuel the growth of MSMEs across the country.

Prashant Muddu

MD & CEO, Jocata

The significance of the MSME sector for the Indian economy is a well acknowledged fact. However, data paucity on the MSME sector often becomes a challenge for industry members and policymakers.

We at SIDBI have been constantly engaged for the development of the sector and have been partnering with institutions to bridge the information asymmetry to enable informed decision-making by stakeholders. It is in this spirit we have created ‘Sumpoorn’ with Jocata, that carries an index measuring economic activity of the MSMEs leveraging the GST data.

The underlying authentic data of GST provides credibility to the Index. With time, the same can be expanded to include more dimensions. I hope the publication shall be of value to the readers and provide a rich perspective on the prevalent trends of the MSME sector.
