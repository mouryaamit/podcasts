What is HSN?

Harmonized System of Nomenclature is a universally accepted uniform code to classify goods in a systematic way developed by World Customs Organization (WCO) and adopted in our GST regime.

Under the Indian GST system, there are 21 sections comprising 99 chapters divided into 1244 headings and 5224 sub-headings.
