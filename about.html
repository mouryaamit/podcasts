Jocata, a wholly owned subsidiary of BillDesk (IndiaIdeas), is a fintech solution provider that aids financial institutions in achieving their Digital Transformation objectives. Jocata powers digital lending, analytics and, financial crime compliance for marquee public and private banks and NBFCs.

As a platform partner for a spectrum of lenders, Jocata found that while technology has proved to be a key enabler, the lack of comprehensive and authentic data poses a major hurdle in bridging the MSME credit gap. This led to the creation of Jocata GST DNA, an MSME credit score that leverages consent-led data from GSTIN and holistically captures the business’ performance. The score has been back tested on over 50,000 MSMEs at leading institutions, who have adopted it for MSME acquisition, underwriting and monitoring.

Jocata Sumpoorn is an extension of this effort to provide a data-driven and timely understanding of MSMEs to further credit penetration to this sector and fuel the MSME powerhouse of the Indian economy.

Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) established in 1990, is the Principal Financial Institution engaged in executing the triple agenda of promotion, financing & development of the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) sector and coordination of the functions of the various institutions engaged in similar activities.

SIDBI partnered with Jocata for its MSME credit score - Jocata GST DNA to enhance its credit underwriting and decisioning using cutting edge technology and data analytics and reduce end-to-end time-to-credit for these borrowers.

SIDBI encouraged Jocata’s efforts towards solving an ecosystem problem – the knowledge deficit in lending to MSMEs and are the premier patrons of the Jocata Sumpoorn Index.
